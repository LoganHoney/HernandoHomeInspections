What Is It

What's Included in a
Full Home Inspection

Our full home inspection is a non-invasive visual walk-through of the property's accessible systems and components. We provide buyers and sellers throughout Hernando County with a detailed digital report — complete with photos — that gives you a clear picture of the home's condition before you commit.

This is not a pass/fail test. It is a professional opinion of the home's condition at the time of inspection, delivered in plain language so you can make informed decisions — whether you're buying in Spring Hill, selling in Brooksville, or anywhere across Hernando County.

Exterior & Roofing

A full review of the exterior wall coverings and roofing system — including surface materials, connections, penetrations, and drainage. We visually evaluate every accessible component for signs of damage, deterioration, or improper installation.

Attic

Our attic inspection covers roof framing, trusses, and sheathing for proper installation and condition, as well as insulation depth and ventilation effectiveness. The attic often reveals what can't be seen from the exterior — and we document it all.

Plumbing

We evaluate visible domestic water supply lines, faucets, fixtures, valves, and waste/vent piping. This includes testing water pressure at exterior hose bibs and identifying the location of the main water shut-off — critical information for any homeowner.

Electrical

Our electrical examination includes the main service panel, capacity, and grounding sources. We test a representative number of outlets and switches to check for proper installation and GFCI protection — one of the most common safety deficiencies found during Hernando County home inspections.

HVAC

We perform a functional review of the heating and cooling systems — including temperature differentials, filter condition, and visible ductwork. In Florida's climate, a properly functioning HVAC system is essential and one of the most scrutinized systems on any inspection.

Interior Components

The interior portion of our inspection covers walls, ceilings, floors, windows, doors, and built-in appliances using normal operating controls. We look for signs of moisture, settlement, improper installation, and general condition throughout every accessible room.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions
About Full Home Inspections

Do I need to be present for the inspection?

While not required, we highly recommend attending. Your inspector can walk you through findings in real time and answer questions about the property's condition on the spot.

Is a home inspection a pass or fail?

No. It is a professional opinion on the condition of the home at the time of inspection — designed to help you make an informed decision, not to approve or reject a purchase.

What if the report reveals problems?

No house is perfect. Our findings give you the information to consult specialists, negotiate repairs, or make confident decisions — that is exactly what the inspection is designed to do.

How quickly will I receive my report?

Same-day delivery is our standard. Your comprehensive digital report with photos is typically delivered the same day as the inspection — so you are never the bottleneck in the transaction.

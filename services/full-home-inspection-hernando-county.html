What’s Included in a Full Home Inspection


Our home inspection is intended to give the buyer a better understanding of what they are purchasing through a non-invasive visual walk-through. We provide detailed digital reports with photos to help you make an informed decision on properties in Brooksville, Spring Hill, and Weeki Wachee.


Exterior & Roofing

A full review of the exterior wall coverings and the roofing system, including surface materials, connections, penetrations, and drainage. We visually review these components for signs of damage or deterioration.


Attic

Our inspection includes a review of roof framing, trusses, and sheathing for proper installation and condition, as well as an evaluation of attic insulation and ventilation.


Plumbing

We evaluate visible domestic water supply lines, faucets, fixtures, valves, and waste/vent piping. This includes testing water pressure at exterior hose bibs and identifying the location of the main water shut-off.


Electric

Our examination includes the main service panel, capacity (available amperage), and grounding sources. We test a representative number of outlets and switches to check for proper installation and GFCI protection.


HVAC

We perform a basic function review of the heating and air conditioning systems, including temperature differentials and filter condition.



Interior Components

The interior portion of our inspection covers walls, ceilings, floors, and built-in appliances using normal operating controls.

What if the report reveals problems?

No house is perfect. Our recommendations are professional opinions on the conditions present so you can consult with specialists for repairs or further evaluation.

Your Lender Requires It.
We Coordinate It.

If you're purchasing a home with an FHA-backed loan, your lender will require an HUD inspection before approving financing. We partner with a licensed structural engineer to provide the report — so you get a credentialed, lender-ready document without having to track down a specialist yourself.

One call to us gets the process started. The structural engineer performs the inspection, the report is delivered within 3 business days, and your lender has what they need to move forward. We serve all of Hernando County including Spring Hill, Brooksville, Weeki Wachee, and Hernando Beach.

About HUD & FHA Inspections

Is an FHA inspection the same as a home inspection?

No. An FHA inspection is done for your lender. A full home inspection is done for you. Both are recommended — and we can coordinate both together.

What happens if the property doesn't pass?

We document the deficiencies clearly so the seller knows exactly what needs to be addressed before your loan can move forward.

How quickly will I get the report?

Within 3 business days. Your lender-ready report is delivered promptly so your timeline doesn't slip.

Do you serve all of Hernando County?

Yes — Spring Hill, Brooksville, Weeki Wachee, Hernando Beach, Hudson, and surrounding communities. We also serve Pasco & Citrus County.

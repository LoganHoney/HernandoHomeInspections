Comprehensive Pool & Spa Evaluation


Our pool inspection is a non-invasive, visual examination of the pool/spa and its associated mechanical equipment. We look for signs of significant non-performance, safety hazards, and unusual wear that could lead to costly repairs for homeowners in Hernando and Pasco Counties.

Safety & Barrier Requirements

Safety is our top priority. We inspect for required safety barriers, including child-safe fences, self-closing/self-latching gates, and functional door alarms on all entries leading to the pool area. We also verify the presence of anti-entrapment drain covers.

Vessel & Decking

We evaluate the pool shell (marcite/finish) for signs of staining, pitting, or cracking. We also review the "cool deck" or paver surfaces, coping, and tile lines for displacement, trip hazards, or grout deterioration.

Mechanical & Filtration

Our inspection includes a functional test of the primary circulation pump and motor. We examine the filter (DE, Sand, or Cartridge) for leaks and pressure gauge functionality, and review the visible plumbing lines for secure mounting and signs of salt-air corrosion.

Electrical & Heating

We test the underwater pool lights and verify the presence of GFCI protection for all pool-related electrical circuits. If a heater (Heat Pump or Gas) is present, we perform a basic function test to ensure it responds to normal operating controls.

Why Get a Professional Pool Inspection?


  • Identify active leaks in pumps, filters, and chlorinators
  • Verify compliance with Florida Residential Swimming Pool Safety Acts
  • Ensure electrical components are safely grounded and bonded

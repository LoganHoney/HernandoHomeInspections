One-Stop Scheduling

Your Home Inspection
and WDO — Same Day

Real estate transactions are stressful enough without coordinating multiple vendors on different days. When you book your home inspection with us, just let us know you need a WDO inspection too — we'll handle the coordination with a licensed pest control company and do our best to get everything done in a single visit.

You make one call. We take it from there. The WDO inspection is performed by and remains the sole professional responsibility of the licensed pest control operator — but you don't have to track them down yourself.

We can't guarantee same-day availability every time, but coordinating this for our clients is something we do regularly and take seriously. It's one less thing on your plate during a busy closing period.

Hernando Home Inspections does not perform WDO inspections, does not issue WDO reports, and is not affiliated with any pest control company. The WDO inspection and its findings are performed by and remain the sole professional responsibility of the licensed pest control operator. Any referral provided is informational only.
